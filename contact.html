FAQ

Common Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

It depends on scope. A focused brand identity project typically runs 6–10 weeks. A full product design and build engagement is usually 3–6 months. We will always give you a clear timeline before we start.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes — some of our best work has been with early-stage companies. We adapt our approach to your stage and budget. If you are pre-seed, reach out and let us see what's possible.

How do you price your services?

We use a mix of fixed-price project fees and day-rate retainers, depending on the engagement type. We are always transparent about costs upfront — no surprises.

What happens after the project launches?

We offer ongoing support retainers, growth optimisation partnerships, and ad-hoc sprint work. Many of our clients have been with us for 3+ years. We are built for long-term relationships, not one-off projects.

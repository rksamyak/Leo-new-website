  • Lead user research, synthesis, and design exploration for enterprise-scale products
  • Produce high-fidelity prototypes and component specifications using Figma
  • Present and defend design decisions to senior stakeholders with clarity and confidence
  • Mentor junior designers and contribute to our internal design practice

  • Architect and implement scalable frontend solutions using modern frameworks and tooling
  • Champion performance, accessibility, and web standards across all deliverables
  • Collaborate closely with design teams to bring complex interactions to life
  • Lead technical discovery, estimations, and architectural reviews for new projects
Apply Now

  • Build and manage client relationships across multiple simultaneous engagements

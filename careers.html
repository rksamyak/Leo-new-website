Our Culture

How We Work

We are a remote-first team that spans 8 countries. We hire for excellence, not geography. What unites us is a shared obsession with making great work and a belief that the best ideas win — regardless of who has them.

We do not have a rigid 9-to-5. We trust people to manage their time, meet their commitments, and do their best work. We have async-first communication and intentional meeting culture — we protect deep work time.

Twice a year, the whole company comes together in person — usually somewhere interesting — for a few days of collaboration, strategy, and genuine connection.

Who We Are

Built on Bold Ideas,
Driven by Results

Leo Digital is a full-service digital agency founded in London in 2014. We partner with brands that want to lead — not follow — and we bring the craft, strategy, and engineering to make that happen.

A decade of digital mastery

Leo Digital was founded with a singular vision: to create digital experiences that don't just look beautiful, but fundamentally transform how businesses connect with their audiences. We are a full-service agency built on craft, strategy, and relentless execution.

From ambitious startups to global enterprises, we bring the same level of dedication to every project — a deep commitment to understanding your business, your users, and the intersection where great design meets meaningful technology.

Our multidisciplinary team of strategists, designers, and engineers work in close collaboration, challenging conventional thinking to deliver work that sets new standards in every industry we touch.

We believe the best digital work is invisible — it just works, delights, and converts. That belief drives every decision we make, from the first discovery session to final deployment and beyond.
